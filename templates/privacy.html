Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Data We Collect

We collect only the information you provide to us, including expense data such as amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions. We also collect basic account information like your email address when you register.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the Spendly service. We use your expense information to generate reports, categorize spending, and help you understand your financial patterns. Your email is used for account authentication and communication.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, loss, or destruction.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. We may use third-party services for hosting and infrastructure, but these providers are bound by confidentiality agreements.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the app or at support@spendly.com.
